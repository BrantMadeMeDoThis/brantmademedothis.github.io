About Us

He Said They Said is the brainchild collaborative group of Happy Valley Improv’s James Tierney and Dawn Rosenbaum. This two-person longform group focuses on creating worlds with big characters that end up colliding in hilarious ways. It all begins with a rumor given by a member of the audience, and it quickly spirals out of control to inspire the amazing world the players create. The characters themselves can and will be played by either person during the course of the show.
